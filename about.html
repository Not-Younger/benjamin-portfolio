About

Hey! I'm Benjamin.

I'm 22-year-old graduate from Southern New Hampshire University with a degree in Marketing.

What began as a hobby has flourished into a dedicated pursuit of excellence in photography and videography.

I specialize in capturing moments and crafting stories through my lens, bringing a unique perspective to every project. My expertise extends to video editing, where I transform raw footage into captivating narratives with creativity and precision.

From stunning landscapes to engaging video content and high-quality real estate visuals, my work reflects my commitment to artistic expression.
